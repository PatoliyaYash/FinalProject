Upcoming Events

Snow Park

Prepare to be enchanted by the upcoming Snow Park in Wonderland, a frost-kissed realm where winter's embrace welcomes all with open arms. Delight in the anticipation of gliding across pristine slopes, crafting intricate snow sculptures, and reveling in the joy of laughter-filled snowball battles.

As the snowflakes dance and the air fills with anticipation, immerse yourself in a world where adventure knows no bounds, and every moment is infused with the magic of the season.

Whether you're a seasoned skier or a first-time snow adventurer, the Snow Park promises thrills, laughter, and unforgettable memories for all who venture into its wintry domain.

Art Gallery

Step through the ornate gates of the forthcoming Wonderland Art Gallery and prepare to embark on an extraordinary journey through a realm where imagination reigns supreme. Located in the heart of Wonderland, this soon-to-be-unveiled gallery promises to be a beacon of creativity, showcasing a mesmerizing array of artworks that blur the lines between reality and fantasy.

From the whimsical to the profound, visitors will be transported to a world where every brushstroke and sculpture tells a story, inviting them to explore the boundless depths of artistic expression.

With its grand opening on the horizon, anticipation mounts as art enthusiasts eagerly await the unveiling of this enchanting sanctuary where dreams take flight and inspiration knows no limits.
