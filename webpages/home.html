Customer Review

John Smith

Fantastic attractions and dining areas. Expect wait times of an hour for most of the big coasters and many of the other attractions. Fast pass is the way to go, but it’s extremely cost prohibitive. I would suggest a lower visitor limit for each day and a slight increase in price to offset the cost for the park.

Michael Johnson

This wonderland has great roller coasters. The layout and other attractions at the park are sub-par from others.... but you go here if you are a roller coaster enthusiast. The food is so-so. It is a far distance from downtown Toronto - allow 45-60 minutes by car. Uber drop off and pick up are convenient. Staff are friendly and helpful.
